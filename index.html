SERVER RULES

Read carefully — ignorance is not an excuse

💬 Discord Rules

  • No spamming of any kind in any channel
  • Do not advertise other servers via channels or DMs
  • NSFW and NSFL content will not be tolerated at any level
  • Use the correct channels — keep topics where they belong
  • No pinging staff without a valid reason
  • Decisions by staff are final — appeal respectfully

🌿 Server / Chat Rules

  • No form of spamming is tolerated in chat
  • No derogatory or discriminative language of any kind
  • Advertising of any kind is NOT allowed
  • Show respect to all users at all times
  • Leaking of ANY personal information is NOT allowed
  • No DDoS / Dox threats of any kind
  • Excessive toxicity is not permitted — Mod's discretion applies

🔶 Gameplay Rules

  • Inappropriate skins or usernames are NOT allowed
  • Do NOT use any software to gain an unfair advantage
  • Any form of boosting or item-generation software is banned
  • Providing falsified evidence to punish a player is NOT allowed
  • No exploiting gen mechanics or duplication bugs
  • Gen griefing or tampering with other players' gens is NOT allowed
  • Report bugs immediately — abusing them results in a ban

⚔ Combat Rules

  • No hacking or use of combat-advantage clients
  • 3v1 are allowed — 4v1 are NOT permitted
  • No spam killing the same person repeatedly
  • No farming players for kills or stats
  • No combat logging — face your fight
  • No spawn camping or spawn trapping of any kind

🔨 Mace & Weapons Rules

  • Custom maces must only be used in designated PVP zones
  • No using mace exploits or unintended weapon combos
  • Do NOT trade or sell custom weapons for real money
  • Weapon duplication of ANY kind results in a permanent ban
  • Mace PVP rank farming is strictly prohibited
  • Wintrading to gain weapon-related ranks is NOT allowed

🚫 Banned Modifications

  • Kill-Aura / Combat Hacks — instant ban
  • X-Ray / OreRadar — ruins economy and gen balance
  • Fly Hacks — any illegitimate flight is prohibited
  • Speed Hacks — movement exploits are not tolerated
  • Auto-Clicker / Macro software — unfair PVP advantage
  • Wurst / Impact / Sigma / Aristois — zero tolerance clients
  • ✅ Allowed: Optifine, Sodium, Iris, Lithium, Xaero's Minimap

These rules are subject to modification at any time — @everyone will be notified.
